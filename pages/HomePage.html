How It Works

Get started with HealthLine in four simple steps. Our platform is designed to make healthcare easy and accessible from the comfort of your home.

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your account in just a few minutes. All it takes is your basic information.

Step 2: Book an Appointment

Select a doctor and choose a time that fits your schedule for your consultation.

Step 3: Video Consultation

Meet with your doctor through a secure and encrypted video call from anywhere.

Step 4: Receive Prescription

Get your prescription instantly after the consultation and manage it online.
